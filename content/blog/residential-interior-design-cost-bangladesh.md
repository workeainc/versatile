## Start with scope, not a square-foot number

Interior design cost depends less on the apartment size and more on how much of the space needs to be planned, detailed, sourced, and executed. A living room refresh is not the same as a full apartment renovation with built-in storage, lighting, civil work, furniture, and site supervision. If you are planning a full home, start with the [residential interior design service](/services/residential) so the scope is clear before numbers are discussed.

Most budgets shift because of three decisions: how much custom work is needed, what materials are selected, and how tightly the execution must be managed.

## What usually affects the budget

- Design development and technical drawings
- Custom cabinetry, wardrobes, TV units, and kitchen storage
- Flooring, wall finishes, ceiling work, and lighting
- Loose furniture, curtains, rugs, and decor
- Electrical, plumbing, and site preparation
- Project management and contractor coordination

## Why Bangladesh projects need local planning

Homes in Bangladesh often need careful decisions around heat, humidity, dust, maintenance, and storage. A beautiful material that fails in daily use is not a premium choice. Good design balances finish, durability, availability, and repairability.

For a deeper look at finish selection, read our guide to [choosing interior materials for humid climates](/blog/materials-for-humid-climates). If your project is location-specific, our [Dhaka interior design page](/interior-design-dhaka) explains how local conditions influence planning.

## How to plan a smarter budget

Decide what must be excellent first. For many homes, that means storage, lighting, circulation, and the rooms used every day. Decorative layers can be phased later, but weak planning is expensive to correct after construction begins.

> A thoughtful budget is not about spending less everywhere. It is about spending clearly where the space will be used the most.

## A simple way to begin

Before asking for a quotation, prepare your floor plan, a few reference images, an approximate timeline, and a realistic comfort range for investment. That helps the studio recommend the right level of design and execution support.
