# Project Case Study Upgrade Standard

Future project pages should read like design journals, not simple portfolio entries.

## Recommended Structure

1. Client Challenge
2. Existing Condition
3. Concept Development
4. Material Selection
5. Lighting Strategy
6. Construction and Site Coordination
7. Final Photography
8. Results
9. Lessons Learned

## Required Content

- Client type and project location
- Clear design goal
- Constraints and site realities
- Material decisions and why they were chosen
- Lighting approach
- Storage or operational planning where relevant
- Timeline and completion date
- Services provided
- Minimum 6 strong photos when available
- Client quote only with permission

## Before / After

Add before/after content only when real project photos are available.

Recommended format:

- Before image
- After image
- Short explanation of what changed
- Alt text describing both states

Do not fake before/after images with stock photography.

## Video

When available, embed a 30-60 second walkthrough from YouTube or a hosted video source.

The video should show:

- Entry sequence
- Main space
- Key material details
- Lighting moments
- Storage or operational details

## Internal Links

Each project should link to:

- Relevant service page
- Relevant blog articles
- Relevant local landing page
- Consultation page

Example:

- Residential project in Gulshan:
  - `/services/residential`
  - `/blog/residential-interior-design-cost-bangladesh`
  - `/blog/materials-for-humid-climates`
  - `/interior-design-gulshan`
  - `/request-consultation`
