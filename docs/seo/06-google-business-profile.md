# 06 - Google Business Profile

Purpose: build local search trust. For interior design searches in Dhaka, Google Business Profile can influence discovery as much as the website.

## Setup Checklist

- Business name: confirm exact official name.
- Primary category: Interior designer.
- Secondary categories: Interior decorator, Architecture firm, Design agency, Office refurbishment service, or similar only if accurate.
- Website: `https://versatileinterior.com.bd`
- Phone: confirm official number before publishing.
- Address: confirm whether public office address or service-area business is preferred.
- Service areas: Dhaka, Gulshan, Banani, Dhanmondi, Bangladesh, and any real served cities.
- Hours: confirm real hours.
- Logo: upload clean logo.
- Cover image: use strong real project image when available.
- Photos: add 15-20 real project images over time.
- Services: add residential, commercial, restaurant, showroom, event/exhibition.
- Business description: write clear, local, non-spammy copy.

## Suggested Business Description

Versatile Interior is a Bangladesh-based interior design studio working across homes, workplaces, restaurants, showrooms, and event environments. The studio focuses on practical planning, material durability, lighting, storage, service flow, and site-ready execution for spaces in Dhaka and beyond.

Before publishing, replace this with final approved business language.

## Review Collection Process

After each completed project:

1. Ask for permission to request a review.
2. Send the Google review link.
3. Ask the client to mention the project type and location naturally.
4. Reply professionally to every review.

Do not incentivize fake reviews.
