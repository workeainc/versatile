# 07 - Backlink Strategy

Purpose: earn relevant authority signals without spam. Quality matters more than quantity.

## Link Targets

Prioritize links to:

- Homepage for brand authority.
- `/interior-design-dhaka` for local authority.
- Key service pages for commercial intent.
- Strong project case studies when real images are published.
- Useful blog guides when they answer specific planning questions.

## Best Link Sources

| Source type | Examples | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Local business directories | Bangladesh business directories, Clutch, design directories | High |
| Google Business Profile | Website link, posts, services | High |
| Suppliers and vendors | Furniture, lighting, material, contractor partners | High |
| Architects/builders | Project collaboration mentions | Medium |
| Design publications | Project features, interviews, photo essays | High |
| Local news/business media | Studio launch, notable project, expert commentary | Medium |
| Client websites | Commercial clients linking to completed interior project | Medium |
| Social profiles | Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Behance, Pinterest | Medium |

## Outreach Template

Subject: Project feature or collaboration mention

Hello [Name],

We recently completed/are documenting [project or collaboration]. We are preparing a short project story with photography and would like to credit relevant collaborators. If useful, we can also provide a short description and images for your website or profile.

Best,  
Versatile Interior

## Rules

- Do not buy low-quality backlink packages.
- Do not use private blog networks.
- Do not publish spun guest posts.
- Do not create fake directory profiles.
- Prefer fewer, more relevant mentions.

## Monthly Goal

Earn or build 2-4 legitimate citations or links per month for the first 6 months.
