## Page Ownership Rules

- Homepage owns broad studio/company intent.
- Local pages own city or area search intent.
- Service pages own service category intent.
- Blog posts own educational and cost-planning intent.
- Project pages own proof, portfolio, and conversion support rather than broad keyword rankings.

## Do Not Target Separately Yet

Avoid creating separate pages for these until there is real content depth:

- luxury interior design Dhaka
- best interior designer Dhaka
- low cost interior design Bangladesh
- furniture design Dhaka
- kitchen interior design Dhaka

These can be mentioned naturally, but they should not become standalone pages until the studio has real examples and a clear offer.
