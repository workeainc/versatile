# 02 - Content Calendar

Purpose: publish consistently without weakening quality. The goal is authority, not volume.

## Publishing Rhythm

- 2 high-quality blog articles per month.
- 1 project case study every 1-2 months when real photos and permission are available.
- 1 content refresh per month after Search Console data starts showing impressions.

## Quality Rules

- Do not publish thin articles under 700 words unless the topic is intentionally narrow.
- Add original examples from Bangladesh projects whenever possible.
- Link to at least one service page and one consultation path.
- Avoid generic global advice that does not mention local climate, budget, materials, site execution, or client decision-making.
- Refresh articles annually or when Search Console shows high impressions but weak click-through.
